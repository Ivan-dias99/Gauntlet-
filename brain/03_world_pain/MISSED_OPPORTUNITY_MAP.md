# MISSED OPPORTUNITY MAP

This file tracks opportunity zones the market still leaves weak or unresolved.

## Opportunity 1 — architect-first creation systems
The market still serves devs, generic teams, or chat users better than principal-architect creators.

## Opportunity 2 — sovereign AI interfaces
Most AI tools are wrappers or assistants, not integrated memory-bearing creation organisms.

## Opportunity 3 — merge-aware creative systems
Most systems do not treat branch/PR/review residue as part of the creator experience problem.

## Opportunity 4 — consequence-bearing memory
Most memory systems either over-store everything or under-preserve what matters.

## Opportunity 5 — creation systems with taste and doctrine
The market underbuilds systems that carry strong structural judgment while remaining usable.

## Ruberra law
Only pursue opportunities that can become inevitable product strength, not mere feature additions.
