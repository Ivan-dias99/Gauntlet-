# IVAN INEVITABILITY MODEL

Inevitable means more than strong.
It means the system feels like the cleanest surviving truth after pressure.

## Signals of inevitability
- fewer moving parts for more power
- stronger continuity across creation, memory, and execution
- less generic than category defaults
- higher usefulness without losing beauty
- uses existing substrate more intelligently than competitors
- makes old fragmented workflows feel obsolete

## Non-inevitable signals
- "good enough"
- trend-following
- decorative innovation
- shallow novelty
- partial coherence
- many options without hard convergence

## Ruberra law
A Ruberra direction is inevitable only if it:
- increases creator power
- reduces fragmentation
- strengthens memory continuity
- improves command and consequence
- absorbs pain that other systems still leave exposed
