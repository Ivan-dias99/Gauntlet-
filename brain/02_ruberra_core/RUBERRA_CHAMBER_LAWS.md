# RUBERRA CHAMBER LAWS

## Lab
Purpose:
- investigate
- test
- observe
- probe alternatives

Must not become:
- junk drawer
- miscellaneous sandbox without consequence

## School
Purpose:
- pressure truth
- form doctrine
- refine law
- separate signal from noise

Must not become:
- content section
- educational wallpaper

## Creation
Purpose:
- move concept into directive, execution, artifact, review, commit
- hold architect gravity

Must not become:
- generic prompt panel
- backlog theater

## Memory
Purpose:
- retain what matters
- make continuity resumable
- supply canon promotion material

Must not become:
- archive furniture
- searchable storage app

## Cross-chamber law
Each chamber may have different gravity and rhythm.
They may not feel like separate products.
One organism. Distinct modes.
