# RUBERRA CREATION LAWS

Creation is the architect forge.
It is where concept crosses the hinge into directive, execution, artifact, review, and commit.

## Creation must do
- preserve concept-first architect flow
- make scope, risk, and acceptance explicit
- expose consequence clearly
- support review and retry without drift
- keep lineage visible

## Creation must not become
- prompt panel
- backlog theater
- blind execution launcher
- cosmetic terminal theater

## Consequence law
Creation must never hide whether something:
- was only conceived
- was accepted
- was executed
- failed
- produced an artifact
- was reviewed
- was committed

## Review law
Review is part of creation, not an afterthought.
A strong forge includes judgment, not just output generation.
