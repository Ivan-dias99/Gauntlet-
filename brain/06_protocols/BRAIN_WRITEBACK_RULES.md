# BRAIN WRITEBACK RULES

## Law
Write back to the brain only when new truth is worth preserving.

## Valid writeback cases
- founder-grade realization
- accepted structural decision
- high-value rejection worth preventing from repeating
- new law protecting Ruberra from drift or weakness
- real pain transformed into product/system discipline

## Invalid writeback cases
- temporary implementation chatter
- low-value summaries
- branch-local noise
- cosmetic notes with no future leverage

## Writeback rule
Converge into repo truth first.
Then decide whether the matter deserves preservation in the brain.

## Anti-bloat note
A bloated brain is a weak brain.
Preserve signal, not residue.
