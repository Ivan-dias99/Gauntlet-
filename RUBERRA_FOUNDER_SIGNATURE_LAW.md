## FOUNDER AUTHORITY

This document records the immutable laws encoded by the sovereign creator of Ruberra.

Every agent, every pioneer, every contributor operates inside these laws — never above them.

---

## LAW I — IDENTITY IS IMMUTABLE

Ruberra has one identity. One spine. One inevitable direction.

It cannot be:
- Rebranded into a generic AI tool
- Forked into a sub-product with different laws
- Diluted by consensus-driven feature addition
- Redefined by market pressure

If identity drift is detected, the identity filter (`runIdentityFilter()`) must fire.
If the filter fires, the surface must be removed, replaced, or subordinated.
There is no third option.

---

## LAW II — THE MISSION IS SOVEREIGN

The mission is the atomic unit of work in Ruberra.

No surface, no feature, no agent, no integration may claim to be the root object of work.
The mission owns everything. Everything serves the mission.

If a new surface does not serve a mission, it has no place in Ruberra.

---

## LAW III — CONSEQUENCE BEFORE DECORATION

Every visible surface must produce consequence or be removed.

A surface that informs but does not act = consequence.
A surface that acts and records = consequence.
A surface that looks important but does nothing = noise.
A surface that mirrors another tool = identity drift.

There is no tolerance for accumulation of noise.

---

## LAW IV — MEMORY IS SACRED

The system does not reset. The system does not forget.

Between sessions, between chambers, between missions — continuity is maintained.
State persists. Context persists. The ledger persists.

Any agent that destroys state without explicit sovereign authorization has violated this law.

## LAW VI — BUILD MUST PASS

`npm run build` is the gate.

No code reaches `main` without a passing build. No exceptions. No "it's just a config change." No "we'll fix it after merge."

The creator signature is encoded in the system's behavior, not its appearance.

- The mission as the atomic unit
- The chambers as sovereign organs

## LAW VIII — ANTI-FRAGMENTATION

No surface exists as an island.

Everything connects:
- Missions connect to chambers
- Chambers connect to the profile ledger
- Pioneers connect to mission routing
- Stacks connect to each other in order
- Every action feeds the continuity fabric

If something is disconnected from the rest of the system, it is either noise (remove) or a premature build (defer).

The visual and interaction standard is flagship.

More Apple Vision Pro than startup SaaS.
More mineral and warm stone than dashboard chrome.
More consequence-bearing interface than notification feed.

If a surface feels consumer, it needs to be elevated or removed.
If a surface feels like a competitor, it needs to be replaced with something sovereign.

---

## LAW X — ONE DIRECTION

Ruberra has one inevitable direction: sovereign mission operating system for operators who build, govern, and compound their intelligence.

There is only one direction. All detours are drift. All shortcuts are regressions.

## SOVEREIGN AUTHORIZATION ACTS

The following changes require explicit sovereign (founder) authorization:

1. Advancing `RUBERRA_CURRENT_PHASE` in `stack-registry.ts`
2. Adding or removing a chamber
3. Adding or removing a stack from the registry
4. Modifying the MOTHER_LAW in `canon-sovereignty.ts`
5. Changing the canonical identity declarations (RUBERRA_IS, RUBERRA_IS_NOT, RUBERRA_CANNOT_BECOME)
6. Merging any PR that contradicts the canonical doc set

All other implementation decisions operate inside the authority of the constitution.
